Cause & Effect of Religion

Be religious V.S. Important for children having faith

The two questions "Are you religious" and "Is it important for children to have faith" are positive correlated as the left figure

World Mapping of Important for children having faith

If we explore further, we can see most countries in Europe and East Asia (almost all high-GDP countries) think it's not so important for children to have faith.

Social Trust V.S. Be religious

Interestingly, we usually think people being religious tend not to cheat others, thus the social trust must higher, but the result is the opposite.

If we look at GDP, the countries lefter and upper, the lower their GDP. Possibly, sufficient social trust is the foundation of higher GDP.
